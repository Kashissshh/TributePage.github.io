A Tribute to the legendary Singer

LATA MANGESHKAR
The Nightingale of India
(28 Sep. 1929 - 6 Feb. 2022)

BIOGRAPHY

Lata Mangeshkar- the woman behind some of the most beautiful songs ever written. Indian singer Lata Mangeshkar is known as the 'Nightingale of India'. Lata ji,was born on September 28, 1929, in Indore, has made significant contributions to both light classical and popular music in India. Lata Mangeshkar Ji was one of the most famous singers in India and beyond. She recorded more than 30,000 songs in a career spanning over seven decades. She has performed Pop, Ghazals, and Bhajans. She even made it into the Guinness Book of Records as the artist with the most albums sold worldwide.The magical voice of Lata ji is crazy about the Indian subcontinent as well as all over the world. Time magazine has acknowledged her as the indispensable and sole empress of Indian playback singing . The Government of India honored her with ' Bharat Ratna in the year 2001'. She died due to complications related to Covid on 6th February 2022, at Breach Candy Hospital, Mumbai . She was ill for some time. Her great singing and melodious voice are crazy all over the world.
